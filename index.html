Visiting Cambridge & Harvard Art Museums

Cambridge is a fun and vibrant neighborhood! We encourage you to explore the place we love calling home, and we would love to give you recommendations - just ask! As for visiting the museum itself, during our cocktail hour, guests are welcome to explore the Harvard Art Museums' first floor galleries. We highly recommend visiting during the daytime for artwork on the upper levels and enjoy the museum's offerings to the fullest - it is open and free to the public from Tuesday to Sunday, 10 AM to 5 PM.

Accommodation Recommendations

For guests traveling from other states / countries, Cambridge has an array of lodging choices conveniently situated near the museum - some closer options are Hotel Veritas (the closest), the Charles Hotel, and Sheraton Commander.

Parking & Local Transportation

Complimentary parking is available at the Broadway Garage adjacent to the museum - please ask Lucy and Charles for a voucher / permit. The museum is just a 5-minute walk from the Harvard Square MBTA Subway station (red line) if you prefer to use public transportation.

Given the venue's capacity limitation, we can only accommodate guests explicitly named on the invitations. If you would really like to bring a +1 / children, please get in touch with us directly (as early as possible).
